Remote work has changed relocation decisions. Many buyers now prioritize space, quiet, and quality of life over commute proximity.

Top Suburbs for Remote Workers

Beaverton  
• Balanced lifestyle  
• Reliable infrastructure

Camas  
• Quiet, polished neighborhoods  
• Strong schools

Vancouver, WA  
• No state income tax  
• Larger homes

Hillsboro  
• Newer housing options

What Matters Most

• Dedicated home office space  
• Internet reliability  
• Access to parks and trails

If you’re remote long-term, lifestyle becomes the deciding factor.
